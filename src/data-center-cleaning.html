Airborne contamination is inevitable in server rooms and data centers. The contamination could emanate from people entering these critical IT environments, equipment being used within these rooms and other sources close to these facilities. As dust settles on IT equipment, it builds up over time and can lead to potential problems such as: corrosion, heat build-up, static conductivity and ultimately equipment failure. Corecta has well trained cleaning specialists who apply strict server room /data center cleaning standards in order to ensure that your IT environments are kept clean at all times. We also conduct indoor air quality (IAQ) assessments for your server rooms and data centers in Ghana and beyond. Our cleaning services include:

  • Raised floor cleaning
  • IT equipment cleaning
  • Overhead area cleaning
  • Subfloor cleaning
  • Post disaster cleaning
  • Post data center construction cleaning
